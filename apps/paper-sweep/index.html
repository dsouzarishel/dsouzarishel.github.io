About Paper Sweep

Paper Sweep helps teachers preserve classroom documents quickly: question papers, answer sheets, grade records, handwritten notes, worksheets, and other pages worth keeping. It runs in the browser, chooses sharp changed frames, and can export either a simple PDF or a ZIP with the PDF, selected images, and nearby non-blurry candidates.
